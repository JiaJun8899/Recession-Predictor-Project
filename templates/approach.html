Our Approach

As the United States is a superpower in the world and US Dollar is the World's Reserve currency. If the United States is in recession, it will surely affect the other countries. As such we decided create an application to predict a recession in the US.

Indicators used:

Consumer price index (CPI) Unemployment Rates Market Yield Rates Industrial Production Rates Treasury Bill Rates Capacity Utilization Rate

Programming language and libraries used:

Front-end:HTML, Javascript and Flask Library in Python
Back-end: Python Libraries used: Pandas Scikit-learn FREDapi Matplot and Plotly
